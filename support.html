Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI conversation assistant work?
Our AI uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to analyze conversation context, your communication style, and the other person's responses to generate personalized message suggestions. The AI learns from successful conversations to improve its recommendations over time.
Is my personal information and conversation data safe?
Yes, we take privacy and security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption. We never share your personal conversations with other users, and you can delete your data at any time. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete details.
What's the difference between free and premium features?
Free users get access to basic AI suggestions and limited daily generations. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited AI generations, advanced personalization options, priority customer support, exclusive conversation templates, and early access to new features.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees apply.
How do I report inappropriate behavior or content?
You can report issues directly through the app using our reporting tools, or contact our Trust & Safety team at githukueliud@gmail.com. We investigate all reports promptly and take appropriate action to maintain a safe community environment.
What devices and platforms are supported?
Flirt Pro is available for iOS (version 13.0+) and Android (version 8.0+) devices. We're continuously working to expand platform support and optimize performance across all supported devices.
